• J U S T I N E L O P E S

Color is one of the most critical aspects of a design. Here are some brand new color combinations to inspire your next project.

Color sets the tone of any visual, engaging viewers while shaping their perceptions. Whether its monochromatic, bright, cool, warm, or complementary, they all play a big role in design.

Finding the right colors for your design can be hard, that's why i'm showcasing these beautiful color combinations for you to use. The color palettes below were inspired by Shutterstock images and the hex codes are provided so you can use them in your own designs!

Monochromatic

Monochromatic color palettes are made up of a single base hue, then extended with that hue's shade, tints, and tones. Adding black, white, or grey to a color, you can create a versatile color palette thats easy on the eyes.
